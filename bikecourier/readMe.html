Where When Why What Who

Developer: Coleman Dietsch

Here in Saint Paul, we have limited options for dedicated bicycle courier services. Not only are there hardly any bicycle couriers, but none of the businesses that I found seemed to offer a form to request new jobs on their websites (besides a contact us for a quote form).

This site will be a place where users can create postings for delivery jobs that they need done. The users will create a new job post that includes the job contact info (name, email, phone), pickup address, delivery address, cargo description (like product, quantity, weight, volume, etc). The users will be required to take photos to verify item descriptions. I think for now I will have the user put in the cash payment they are offering for the job but if I have time, I would like to come up with an algorithm that takes the distance between the pickup and delivery addresses and multiplies it by an amount to calculate payments instead.

This is for local people in Saint Paul who have time sensitive deliveries. Bicycle couriers have advantages for getting around busy parts of the city, especially during peak rush hour traffic.

Job Request Rules

  1. Cargo must be small enough to fit into the backpack of the bicycle courier.
  2. The site and our couriers have the right to decline a job at any point if it is deemed unsafe or illegal.
  3. Cargo must not be hazardous, toxic, dangerous, or unsafe to transport.
  4. Cargo must be tightly sealed to prevent leaking of solids and liquids. We will not accept leaky cargo.
  5. Cargo must be accurately described.
  6. Cargo must be clearly photographed.
  7. Cargo cannot exceed certain dimensions.
  8. Cargo cannot exceed certain weights.
  9. Cargo cannot exceed certain values.
  10. Only 1 delivery is allowed per job. To request for multiple deliveries, multiple jobs must be requested.

List of Stored Procedures

  2. getInProgressJobs() - Selects relevant data for jobs that are marked as "In Progress"
  3. getNewJobs() - Selects relevant data for jobs that are marked as "New"
